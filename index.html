Save a Stray, Day by Day

Every year, around the world, we can see a vast number of abandoned pets salvaging on the streets. Such a sight warms the hearts of many people causing them to pluck a furry friend from the streets and care for them.
But, how many people actually know how to take care of a stray?
How many people know what to do immediately when they see the stray hurt or at risk?

ABOUT US

We intend to reach a large audience and educate them on how to care for a stray animal, temporarily or permanently by creating awareness and providing resources and material to viewers on management and care of their furry friend.
Our content is easily accessible, incredibly efficient and most importantly, completely free of cost!

Covid-19 on Stray Animals

Strays are being hit the hardest, and those that are healthy enough are starving.With the implementation of lockdown and physical distancing measures, more people are staying home, while restaurants and most markets are closed - both vital food sources for strays!
